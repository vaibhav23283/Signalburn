Response: 🚨 For someone having a stroke:
1. Call for emergency help right away — do not delay.
2. Make sure the person is sitting or lying down in a safe place.
3. Do not give them anything to eat or drink.
4. Loosen any tight clothing around their neck and chest.
5. If they are unconscious, turn them onto their side to keep their airway clear.
6. Stay with the person and try to keep them calm until help arrives.
7. If you can, note the time when the symptoms started — this information is helpful for medical staff.
8. Remember the FAST signs — Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call emergency — and tell the emergency responders about the symptoms you observed.
Call 112 or 108 immediately.
